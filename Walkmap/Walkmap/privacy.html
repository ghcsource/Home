Privacy Policy
When you use Walkmap, we will collect your location information. This information is used to show your trail for a period of time. Walkmap will NOT share your location information with anyone. When you click "Unbinding" button of main UI, Walkmap will stop to collect location information of you.


隐私政策
当您使用Walkmap时，我们会收集您的位置信息。该位置信息用来向您显示过去一段时间您的轨迹。Walkmap不会与任何人共享您的位置信息。当您点击主界面的"解绑"后，Walkmap将会停止收集您的位置信息。
